01/04 Our Story

From Local
Challenge to
Digital Solution

SACCOPay was born from a simple observation: SACCOs in Kenya were struggling with outdated systems while members demanded modern digital experiences.

What started as a small project to help a single SACCO in Nairobi automate their loan disbursements has grown into a comprehensive fintech platform serving cooperatives across East Africa.

To empower Savings and Credit Cooperative Organizations with cutting-edge financial technology that simplifies operations, enhances member experiences, and drives sustainable growth across Africa.
